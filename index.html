Climate Change & Global Warming

Its real. And Its happening.

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's climate system observed since the pre-industrial period due to human activities. Climate change not only involves changes in Earth's temperature, but it also encompasses extreme weather events, rising sea levels, endangering wildlife populations and many other grave effects (NRDC,2016).
